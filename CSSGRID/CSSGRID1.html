About Rachel Andrew

Rachel is a web developer and technical writer and editor. She works for Google on the Chrome team as a senior staff technical writer and content lead for web.dev and developer.chrome.com. She is a member of the CSS Working Group of the W3C, and edits a couple of CSS specifications.

Prior to Google she spent 20 years as a freelancer and business owner. She founded edgeofmyseat.com in 2001 as a web development consultancy, the company moved into products launching Perch CMS in 2009 and Notist in 2018.

Her professional web development skills range from front and back-end development to operations.

You can read and see more of Rachel at her website rachelandrew.co.uk.
